  • A Friendly Introduction to Linear Algebra (June 2025)

    Abstract: An introductory-level textbook in Indonesian that offers an intuitive approach to linear algebra. Covers systems of linear equations, vectors, vector spaces, matrices, and elementary row operations (ERO), with simple explanations and real-life analogies. Collaboration with Sahira AK.

  • A Conceptual Overview of Perelman’s Entropy Formula in Ricci Flow Dynamics (June 2025)
    [Literature Review]
    Alwin

    Abstract: This report reviews Perelman’s 2002 paper on the entropy formula for Ricci flow, outlining the entropy functional W, reduced volume \tilde{V}, and their monotonicity. Key results like the “No Breathers” and “No Local Collapsing” theorems are explained, showing how Perelman’s approach advanced Hamilton’s program toward proving the Geometrization Conjecture.

    Preview
  • FOH-1: Front-Only Hardening for Obscuring API Keys in Serverless Frontends (September 2025)
    [Research Paper]
    Alwin Sahira AK

    Abstract: FOH-1 (Front-Only Hardening) raises the cost of abusing browser-shipped secrets without claiming server-level secrecy. It combines AES-256-GCM sealing, shard-based runtime key reconstruction, JIT decryption, buffer zeroization, light anti-debugging, and deployment-bound ciphertext, with a pure-frontend reference and test plan. Collaboration with Sahira AK.

    Preview

    Abstract: An analysis of the computational complexity of protein folding, focusing on the NP-hardness of structure prediction using the HP lattice model and its implications for computational biology. Collaboration with Sahira AK.

    [Research Paper]
