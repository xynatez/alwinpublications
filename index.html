Selected Publications

  • A Friendly Introduction to Linear Algebra (June 2025)
    [Book]

    Abstract: An introductory-level textbook in Indonesian that offers an intuitive approach to linear algebra. Covers systems of linear equations, vectors, vector spaces, matrices, and elementary row operations (ERO), with simple explanations and real-life analogies. Collaboration with Sahira AK.

  • What Is Protein Folding and Why Is It NP-Hard (June 2025)
    [Research Paper]

    Abstract: An analysis of the computational complexity of protein folding, focusing on the NP-hardness of structure prediction using the HP lattice model and its implications for computational biology. Collaboration with Sahira AK.

  • Comparison of Current Narrow AI Systems and Hypothetical Artificial General Intelligence (July 2025)
    [Research Paper]

    Abstract: This paper analyzes the gap between current narrow AI (ANI) and the concept of AGI, highlighting key differences in generality, autonomy, and understanding. It discusses architectural needs, limitations of current systems, benchmarks for AGI, and alignment challenges.

  • How Shor’s Algorithm Breaks Modern Cryptography (June 2025)
    [Research Paper]

    Abstract: Explains how Shor’s quantum algorithm threatens RSA and ECC by efficiently solving integer factorization and discrete logarithm problems, discusses quantum hardware progress, and the urgency of post-quantum cryptography.

    Download PDF

    Download PDF
  • Understanding the Obstacles to Proving P is not Equal to NP From Relativization to Algebraization and Beyond (June 2025)
    [Research Paper]

    Abstract: This paper explains why proving P ≠ NP is so difficult, focusing on formal barriers such as Relativization, Natural Proofs, and Algebraization. It provides intuitive explanations and mathematical sketches to help readers understand why this fundamental question remains unresolved.
